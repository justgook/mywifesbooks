The Randolph Carter Tales is a collection of H.P. Lovecraft stories focusing on Randolph Carter, a sensitive, intellectual dreamer and presumed alter ego of the author. The tales follow his journey from uncovering occult horrors in New England to exploring the surreal Dreamlands and traversing cosmic, interdimensional realms.
